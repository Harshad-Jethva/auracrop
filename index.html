Solving a Critical Problem for Farmers

A farmer's biggest challenge is crop disease and pests. By the time visual symptoms appear, the damage is already done, leading to yield loss and increased costs. We bridge the gap between problem and prevention.

The Problem

Reactive farming leads to crop loss, excessive pesticide use, and reduced income. Farmers lack the tools to see the invisible, early signs of distress.

Our Solution

AuraCrop's sensor stations and AI platform detect issues at a pre-symptomatic stage, enabling precise, timely intervention and shifting farming to a proactive model.

Societal Benefit

We empower farmers, promote environmental sustainability by reducing chemical usage, and enhance food security for everyone.

Our 5-Step Predictive Process

From data collection to actionable alerts, our process is designed for precision and reliability.

1. Sensing & Data Collection

Our field stations use advanced VOC and multispectral sensors to continuously gather data on plant health and environmental conditions, capturing signals invisible to the human eye.

2. On-Device Pre-Processing

The onboard microcontroller processes raw sensor data, cleaning it and extracting key features to reduce data size and improve efficiency before transmission.

3. Secure Cloud Transmission

The processed data is securely transmitted via Wi-Fi or cellular networks to our central cloud platform, ready for in-depth analysis.

4. AI Analysis & Prediction

Our trained AI models analyze the incoming data streams in real-time, identifying the unique digital signatures of specific diseases, pests, or nutrient deficiencies.

5. Actionable Alerts

When a potential issue is detected, a simple, clear alert is sent directly to the farmer's smartphone, pinpointing the problem and recommending a targeted solution.
